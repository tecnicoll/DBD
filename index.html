"Empowering Individuals. Boosting Local Businesses. Building Communities."

Welcome to Dundee Business Development (DBD), your gateway to unlocking new opportunities and driving positive change in the East of Scotland. As a publicly funded agency, we are dedicated to fostering a thriving local economy by equipping residents with essential skills, facilitating their entry into the job market, and doing this while supporting the growth of small businesses, some of which will create employment opportunites. At DBD, we believe in the power of community viewed as a virtuous circle, where we enhance clients' employability while simultaneously nurturing entrepreneurship in our community. Our targeted, holistic and highly localised service approach ensures that we understand the unique needs of our region, Dundee adn Angus, while placing a strong emphasis on social impact and community development. We invite you to join us on this journey as we empower each other, create local jobs, and build a stronger future together.

To access Dundee Business Development's (DBD) services for skills development and job market facilitation and move to a brighter future peronally or for your business, follow these steps:

Depending on whetether you are coming to us as an indivudal or a business, navigate to either the Business Development Resources/Sign-up Page or Client Resources/Sign-up by clicking below via the picture or clicking above in the menu bar: Look for the dedicated page on the DBD website for either individual clients seeking skills development and job market facilitation. Or for those seeking business devlopment consultancy DBD offers a suite of business services designed to support and empower small local businesses and entrepreneurs.

There you can:

  • Explore the Services: On the Client Resources page, you will find information about the various services and programs offered by DBD. These may include skills training workshops, job placement assistance, career counseling, resume building, interview preparation, and more. On the Business Development resources option we detial services which include mentorship programs, access to funding opportunities, business planning assistance, marketing guidance, and educational workshops on various business topics. DBD also provides expert advice, networking opportunities, a supportive community, and enhanced visibility for businesses within the local community. We can also help in staff trainings.Take the time to read through the details of each service to determine which align with your needs.

  • Sign up or Express Interest: Depending on the specific process implemented by DBD, you may be required to sign up or simply express your interest in accessing their services. Fill out the online registration form and provide your contact information. Follow the instructions provided to initiate the sign-up process.

  • Attend Orientation or Assessment: Once you have expressed your interest in DBD's services, you may be invited to attend an orientation session or an assessment to determine your specific needs and goals. This step helps DBD tailor their services to your specific requirements.

  • Engage with DBD's Resources and Support:After completing the necessary registration and assessment procedures, you will gain access to DBD's resources, expertise and support. This may include attending workshops, receiving one-on-one coaching or mentoring, accessing job listings, and utilizing various tools and resources to enhance your skills and improve your employability or business acumen.

  • Stay Connected: It's important to maintain communication with DBD throughout your engagement with their services. They may provide updates, additional resources, or new opportunities that are relevant to your goals. Stay connected through email, phone, or any other communication channels provided by DBD. People and organisations are always learning and devlopmeing. And after you have found employment, or move to develop and expand you business we can still offer support, mentoring and advice, even possible networking and useful contacts and meet-ups.
